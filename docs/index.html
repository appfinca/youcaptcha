A free CAPTCHA service that benefits YOU

SERVE CHALLENGES

Show YouCAPTCHA challenges on your website to stop spam while letting your valid users pass through with ease.

EARN COINS

Every solved challenge produces 2 coins. One for you, another for the solver (your website user).

GET REWARDS

At the end of every month, you can either cash out your coins or turn them into trees on the earth.


You decide the value

While other CAPTCHA service providers use human bandwidth to annotate images (to train their own AI programs) and leave you little control of the created value, YouCAPTCHA gives the value and control back to you. We partner with brands to display products that might be interesting to your audience as challenge images, and make money when people see, click, or buy the products. Every month, we distribute our revenue in proportion to all coin holders. You can decide how to use your share through the YouCAPTCHA dashboard:

DIRECT DEPOSIT

If you choose this option, we’ll deposit what you’ve earned into your bank account or debit card at the end of every calendar month. Payments vary based on how much we earn from the brands and how many challenges solved by users on your website.

TREE PLANTING

We also partner with tree-planting organizations around the world, such as the Trees.org, so you can turn your coins into real trees. By choosing this option you have a direct impact on the earth and lives of families in our rural community.

YouCAPTCHA provides a widget that you can embed into your website to show how many trees you've grown to your users.

YouCAPTCHA partners with tree-planting organizations around the world to turn your coins into trees.

We support planting fast-growing soil rebuilding trees, fruit trees that diversify incomes and nutrition, and tress that can provide forage and fuel-wood.

These trees promote the long-term health of the land and change lives of people forever.
